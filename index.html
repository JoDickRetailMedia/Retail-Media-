About Us

Retail Media Australia is a neutral, independent organisation committed to:

  • Defining measurement and attribution standards
  • Educating agencies and advertisers
  • Providing market intelligence and category sizing
  • Connecting stakeholders through thought leadership and advocacy

Founding members will help shape the measurement frameworks, education agenda, and narrative around the value of retail media.
